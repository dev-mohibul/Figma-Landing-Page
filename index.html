Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

What is the GxM score?

The GxM (Gold Equivalent Multiplier) score is our proprietary metric that converts drilling results across all commodities into a standardized gold equivalent value. This allows for direct comparison of results from different commodities and helps identify the most significant discoveries.

How quickly are alerts delivered after an ASX release?

Our AI system typically delivers alerts within seconds of an ASX announcement being published. This is significantly faster than the 20-minute delayed data most retail investors receive, giving you a substantial time advantage.

Can companies pay to be featured in alerts?

No. Our system is 100% automated and based on quantified metrics. Companies cannot influence or pay to be included in our alerts. This ensures complete independence and objectivity in our service.

What commodities do you cover?

We cover 30+ commodities including precious metals (gold, silver), base metals (copper, nickel, zinc), battery metals (lithium, cobalt, graphite), and other strategic minerals. Our GxM scoring system allows us to compare results across all these commodities.
